Our Story

Born from the 2025 LA Wildfires

In January 2025, thousands of families across the Palisades and Altadena lost everything. Wedding albums, baby pictures, generations of memories — gone in minutes.

Max's Memories was founded on a simple conviction: this loss is preventable. Every shoebox of photos sitting in a closet is a ticking clock. We're here to stop it.

Every photo preserved. Every memory protected.

How It Works

Simple. Personal. Professional.

1

Meet

I come to your home for a 15-minute consultation. We go through your photos together and talk about how you'd like them organized.

2

Scan

I take your photos to my studio. Every photo is scanned front and back at 1200 DPI with professional equipment.

3

Enhance

AI restoration brings faded photos back to life. Dates from handwriting on the backs are extracted and embedded in the metadata.

4

Return

2-3 days later I'm back at your door with organized archival sleeves, a fireproof case, your digital collection, and a personal walkthrough.
